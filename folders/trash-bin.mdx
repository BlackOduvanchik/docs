## Overview

The **Trash Bin** holds deleted profiles for 30 days before they are permanently removed. During this period, you can restore profiles to any folder.

{/* Screenshot: Trash Bin folder with deleted profiles */}

## Restoring a profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Trash Bin">
    Click **Trash Bin** in the sidebar folder list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select profiles to restore">
    Use checkboxes to select one or more profiles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Restore">
    Click the **Restore** button in the action bar. Choose a destination folder.

    {/* Screenshot: restore button and folder picker */}
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Permanent deletion

Profiles in the Trash Bin are automatically deleted after **30 days**. To delete immediately:

1. Open the Trash Bin
2. Select the profiles
3. Click **Delete Permanently**

<Warning>
  Permanent deletion cannot be undone. All profile data including fingerprint, cookies, and history is destroyed.
</Warning>

## Empty the Trash Bin

To delete all profiles in the Trash Bin at once, click the **Empty Trash** button at the top of the Trash Bin view.
