## Overview

Transfer proxies to team members or other Oxygen Atmos users without manually re-entering credentials. Transferred proxies appear in the recipient's proxy manager.

## Transfer proxies

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select proxies">
    In the proxy manager, select one or more proxies using checkboxes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Transfer">
    Click **Transfer** in the action bar.

    {/* Screenshot: proxy transfer button */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter recipient">
    Enter the recipient's Oxygen Atmos username or email.

    {/* Screenshot: proxy transfer dialog */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose transfer mode">
    | Mode | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Copy** | Recipient gets a copy; you keep the original |
    | **Move** | Proxy is removed from your account and sent to the recipient |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click **Send** to complete the transfer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  When using **Move** mode, any profiles assigned to the transferred proxy will lose their proxy assignment. Reassign proxies to affected profiles after the transfer.
</Warning>
