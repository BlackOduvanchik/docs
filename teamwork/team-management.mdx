## Team member list

Navigate to **Teamwork** → select your team → **Members** to see all team members with their roles and status.

{/* Screenshot: team members list */}

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Member's display name |
| **Email** | Account email |
| **Role** | Owner, Admin, or Member |
| **Status** | Active, Pending (invited but not accepted) |
| **Joined** | Date the member joined the team |

## Change a member's role

1. Click on the member's row in the member list
2. Select a new role from the **Role** dropdown
3. Click **Save**

### Role capabilities

| Action | Owner | Admin | Member |
|---|---|---|---|
| Manage team settings | Yes | Yes | No |
| Invite/remove members | Yes | Yes | No |
| Create shared folders | Yes | Yes | No |
| Launch shared profiles | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Edit shared profiles | Yes | Yes | Per permission |
| View shared profiles | Yes | Yes | Per permission |

## Remove a member

1. Go to the member list
2. Click the **Remove** button next to the member
3. Confirm the removal

<Info>
  Removed members immediately lose access to all shared profiles and proxies. Any profiles they launched will be stopped.
</Info>

## Leave a team

If you're a member (not the owner), you can leave a team:

1. Go to **Teamwork** → select the team
2. Click **Leave Team**
3. Confirm

<Warning>
  The team owner cannot leave the team. Transfer ownership first, then leave.
</Warning>
