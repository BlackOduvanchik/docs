## Send an invitation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the team">
    Navigate to **Teamwork** → select your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click the **Invite Member** button.

    {/* Screenshot: invite member button */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email or username">
    Enter the invitee's Oxygen Atmos email address or username.

    {/* Screenshot: invite dialog */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set role">
    Choose the role for the new member:

    - **Admin** — can manage team settings and members
    - **Member** — can access shared profiles based on permissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click **Send Invite**. The user receives a notification in their Oxygen Atmos account and via email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invite link

You can also generate a shareable invite link:

1. Go to **Teamwork** → **Settings** → **Invite Link**
2. Click **Generate Link**
3. Copy and share the link with your team members
4. Anyone with the link can join your team with the default role

<Warning>
  Invite links grant access to your team. Only share them with trusted users. You can revoke the link at any time from team settings.
</Warning>

## Pending invitations

View and manage pending invitations in **Teamwork** → **Members** → **Pending** tab.

- **Resend** — send the invitation email again
- **Revoke** — cancel the invitation before it's accepted
