## Overview

The transfer key allows the team owner to transfer full ownership of the team to another member. This is useful when the original owner leaves the organization or wants to delegate control.

## Generate a transfer key

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open team settings">
    Navigate to **Teamwork** → select your team → **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Transfer section">
    Scroll to **Transfer Ownership**.

    {/* Screenshot: transfer ownership section */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate key">
    Click **Generate Transfer Key**. A unique key is created and displayed.

    {/* Screenshot: generated transfer key */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the key">
    Copy the key and send it to the new owner through a secure channel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Accept ownership

The recipient uses the transfer key to claim ownership:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Teamwork">
    Navigate to **Teamwork** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Accept Transfer">
    Click **Accept Transfer** and paste the transfer key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review the transfer details and click **Accept**. Ownership is transferred immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After transfer

- The **new owner** gets full control of the team, all shared folders, and settings
- The **previous owner** is demoted to Admin role
- All other team members and their permissions remain unchanged

<Warning>
  Transfer keys are single-use and expire after 24 hours. Generate a new key if the previous one expired. Once used, the transfer cannot be reversed without the new owner's cooperation.
</Warning>
