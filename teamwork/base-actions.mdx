## Create a team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open team settings">
    Navigate to **Teamwork** in the sidebar.

    {/* Screenshot: teamwork section in sidebar */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Team">
    Click the **Create Team** button.

    {/* Screenshot: create team button */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your team">
    Enter a team name (e.g., "Marketing Team", "Agency A").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    The team is created. You're the owner with full access. Now [invite members](/teamwork/invite-to-team).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Rename a team

1. Go to **Teamwork** → select your team
2. Click the **Settings** icon
3. Update the team name and click **Save**

## Delete a team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open team settings">
    Go to **Teamwork** → select your team → **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Delete Team">
    Scroll down and click **Delete Team**.

    {/* Screenshot: delete team button */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the deletion. All shared access is revoked immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Deleting a team does not delete the profiles. Profiles remain with the owner, but team members lose access immediately.
</Warning>

## Switch between teams

If you belong to multiple teams, use the team selector dropdown in the sidebar to switch between them.
