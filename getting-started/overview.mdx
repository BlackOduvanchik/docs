## Welcome to Oxygen Atmos

Oxygen Atmos is a Chromium-based antidetect browser designed for managing multiple browser profiles with unique digital fingerprints. Each profile operates as an isolated browser environment, making it ideal for affiliate marketing, e-commerce, social media management, and cryptocurrency workflows.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    [Register](/getting-started/registration) for an Oxygen Atmos account to access the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the browser">
    [Download and install](/getting-started/installation) Oxygen Atmos on your machine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a profile">
    Set up your first [browser profile](/browser-profiles/about-profiles) with a unique fingerprint.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a proxy">
    Assign a proxy to your profile for network isolation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
