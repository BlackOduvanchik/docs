## Create an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the registration page">
    Go to [wallet.oxygen.tools/en](https://wallet.oxygen.tools/en) and click **Sign Up**.

    {/* Screenshot: registration page with Sign Up button highlighted */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Fill in your email address and create a password. You can also sign up using a third-party provider if available.

    {/* Screenshot: registration form fields */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link.

    {/* Screenshot: verification email example */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Return to the login page and sign in with your credentials. You'll be taken to the main dashboard.

    {/* Screenshot: dashboard after first login */}
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard with:

- **Profiles list** — all your browser profiles
- **Folders** — organize profiles into groups
- **Proxy manager** — manage your proxy connections
- **Team** — collaboration and sharing settings

{/* Screenshot: main dashboard overview with sidebar navigation */}

## Subscription plans

Oxygen Atmos offers multiple subscription tiers. Visit the pricing page in your dashboard to view available plans and features.

<Info>
  Free tier includes a limited number of profiles. Upgrade to unlock more profiles, team features, and API access.
</Info>
